Everybody has that one book that would have triggered their interest in reading,and for me taht book is "A thousand splendid suns" by Khaled hosseini.

But little did I know i would fall in love with a novel until I read “A Thousand splendid suns" by Khaled hosseini.It has been days I had finished reading the book ,but it has left a great impact on me and I just can't stop talking about this book.Khaled hosseini’s way of narration got my heart wrenched.I have not been a passionate reader but this book made me want to read more.

The plot of this book was just insane I would say.And eventually I fell in lobve with this book.

This book felt raw depicting the real world instances,talking about the personalities of people. As a whole,I surely would recommend this book if u wanna kill time.

I was curious to read this book just because of it's title "A good girl's guide to murder".
